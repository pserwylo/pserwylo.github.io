    Bio

    I've been a lover of free software since the 2000's, attending and speaking at many Linux conferences and Open Source Developer Conferences. Among my day job of being a dad and working as a software developer, I also build and maintain the Lexica, Super Retro Mega Wars, and Beat Feet Android games and the BabyDots app to help babies relax.
