Image placement

In Word, you typically place images manually

With LaTeX, you give "hints" and let LaTeX place them

  • "Place image right here"
  • "Place image at the start of the next page"
  • "Place image right here, but if thats impossible, place it at the start of the next page"

Presenter Notes

  • How often do you: Add image, place carefully, change earlier paragraph, stuff all of your images.
